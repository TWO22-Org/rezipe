# Project Brief

## Purpose
Build ChefStream (Rezipe), an iOS app that turns YouTube cooking videos into structured recipes with translation and curation.

## Scope (MVP)
- iOS app with curated categories + search
- Embedded YouTube player with compliant playback
- Automatic recipe extraction and translation from descriptions/transcripts
- Recipe card UI under the video

## Success Criteria
- Users can reliably find recipe videos and follow a recipe card
- Extraction pipeline returns usable structured recipes for most videos
- App stays compliant with YouTube policies

## Out of Scope (MVP)
- Advanced personalization or social features
- Full web client
- Offline-first beyond cached recipe cards
